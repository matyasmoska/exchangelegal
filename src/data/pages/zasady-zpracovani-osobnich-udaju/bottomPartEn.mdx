## **8. Data Subject Rights**

A data subject who can be identified as a natural or legal person and proves their identity will have the following rights:

Right to access personal data

According to Article 15 of the GDPR, the data subject has the right to access personal data, which includes:

– confirmation of whether we process your personal data,

– information about the purposes of processing, categories of personal data affected, recipients to whom the personal data has been or will be made available, planned processing time, existence of the right to request correction or deletion of personal data related to the data subject, or restriction of their processing, or to object to this processing, the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, all available information about the source of personal data if they were not obtained from the data subject, the fact that automated decision-making, including profiling, is taking place, and the appropriate safeguards in case of data transfer outside the EU,

– in case the rights and freedoms of other persons are not adversely affected, you may request a copy of the personal data.

If a request is made repeatedly, 15zisif.cz is entitled to charge a reasonable fee for providing a copy of personal data.

Right to Rectify Inaccurate Data

According to Article 16 of the GDPR, you have the right to rectify inaccurate personal data that 15zisif.cz processes about you. You are also obliged to notify changes to your personal data and provide proof that such a change has occurred. Additionally, you must cooperate with 15zisif.cz if it is determined that the personal data it processes about you is inaccurate. The correction will be made without undue delay, always with regard to the technical possibilities.

Right to Erasure

According to Article 17 of the GDPR, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data if 15zisif.cz cannot prove legitimate reasons for processing such data. 15zisif.cz has mechanisms in place to ensure the automatic anonymization or deletion of personal data when it is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was processed.

Right to Restrict Processing

According to Article 18 of the GDPR, the data subject has the right to restrict processing until the issue is resolved, if they dispute the accuracy of personal data, the reasons for their processing, or if they object to their processing, and this objection is submitted in writing to the address of 15zisif.cz.

Right to Notification of Correction, Deletion, or Restriction of Processing

According to Article 19 of the GDPR, the data subject has the right to be notified by 15zisif.cz in case of correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data processing. If personal data is corrected or deleted, 15zisif.cz will inform individual recipients, except where this proves impossible or would require disproportionate effort.

Right to Data Portability

According to Article 20 of the GDPR, you have the right to data portability for data that concerns you and that you provided to us as the controller, in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. You also have the right to request that we transfer this data to another controller. If exercising this right could adversely affect the rights and freedoms of third parties, your request cannot be fulfilled.

Right to Object to the Processing of Personal Data

According to Article 21 of the GDPR, you have the right to object to the processing of your personal data by 15zisif.cz. If 15zisif.cz cannot prove that there is a compelling legitimate reason for processing that outweighs the interests or rights and freedoms of the data subject, 15zisif.cz will cease processing based on the objection without undue delay.

Right to Withdraw Consent to Personal Data Processing

If you give 15zisif.cz consent to process your personal data, you may withdraw this consent at any time. The withdrawal must be made explicitly, clearly, and definitively, either in writing to the address of 15zisif.cz or via email at info@15zisif.cz.

Automated Individual Decision-Making, Including Profiling

The data subject has the right not to be subject to any decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which would have legal effects on them or similarly significantly affect them. 15zisif.cz states that it does not carry out automated decision-making without human intervention with legal effects for the data subjects.

Right to Contact the Office for Personal Data Protection

You have the right to file a complaint regarding the processing of your personal data with the Office for Personal Data Protection, Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Prague 7. The website of the office: www.uoou.cz.
